Windows Visual Manual

1

Click Download TeamViewer QS

2

Open the App

Mac Manual

1

Grant permissions

Go to System Preferences -> Security & Privacy -> Accessibility and check TeamViewerQS.

2

ID and Password

The Mac app shows the ID and password just like in Windows.

Security Notice: The connection is one-time only. Once you close the program, we cannot access your computer without a new ID and password, which change every time.

Android Guide

Download the QuickSupport app from the Google Play Store to get started.

Download on: Google Play

The connection is secure. We can only see your device during this call.

1

Install TeamViewer QuickSupport

Open Google Play, search for TeamViewer QuickSupport and tap Install. Once done, tap Open.

2

Accept terms & grant permissions

Tap through the welcome screens and tap Agree and continue. When prompted, allow notifications and display over other apps — these are required for the session.

3

Share your ID

The app shows Your ID — a 9-digit number. Read this to your DigiKaveri helper over the phone so they can connect.

4

Verify & allow access

When the connection request arrives, check the email ends with @digikaveri.com, tick "I trust this person" and tap Allow access. Then tap Start to begin screen sharing.

5

You're connected!

Your screen is now visible to the DigiKaveri helper. If the app asks you to install an add-on or grant accessibility access, follow the prompts — our helper will guide you through.

Android Visual Manual

1

Open the DigiKaveri Guide

On the DigiKaveri website, tap Download on Google Play to open the app in the Play Store.

2

Install TeamViewer QuickSupport

The TeamViewer QuickSupport app page opens in Google Play. Tap Install to download it.

3

Open the App

Once installed, tap Open to launch TeamViewer QuickSupport.

4

Accept Terms and Continue

The Welcome screen appears. Tap Agree and continue to accept the EULA and Data Processing Agreement.

5

Secure Access Info

The app shows a Secure access tip — always verify you trust the person before granting access. Tap Continue.

6

Remote Support Info

The app explains Remote support — you can receive help on any device. Tap Continue.

7

How to Receive Support

The app explains how to share your ID or session code. Tap Continue.

8

File Transfer Info

The app mentions File transfer — you can share files during the session. Tap Let's get started!

9

Allow Notifications

The app asks permission to send notifications. Tap Allow so you receive session alerts.

10

Allow Display Over Other Apps

A prompt appears asking for Display over other apps permission. Tap Settings to grant it.

11

Enable QuickSupport in "Appear on Top"

In your phone's Appear on top settings, find QuickSupport and enable the toggle.

12

Your ID is Ready

The main screen shows Your ID. Share this number with DigiKaveri Support so they can connect to your device.

13

Incoming Connection — Verify the User

When DigiKaveri initiates the connection, a Verify this user screen appears. Make sure you trust the person, then tap Verify user.

14

Confirm DigiKaveri's Identity

Check that the email shown ends with @digikaveri.com. Tick "I trust this person" and tap Allow access.

15

Start Screen Sharing

A Start recording or casting dialog appears. Leave it on Entire screen and tap Start.

16

Allow Location (Optional)

The app may ask for your location. You can tap Only this time — this is optional and does not affect support.

17

Install TeamViewer Add-On

A dialog appears: Install TeamViewer add-on. This is needed for full remote control. Tap Install.

18

Install TeamViewer Universal Add-On

Google Play opens with the TeamViewer Universal Add-On. Tap Install to download it.

19

Add-On Installed

The TeamViewer Universal Add-On now shows as Installed. Go back to the QuickSupport app.

20

Enable the Universal Add-On

A dialog explains the Universal Add-On activation — it enables full control during the session. Tap Enable.

21

Open Accessibility > Installed Apps

Your phone's Accessibility settings open. Tap Installed apps to find the add-on.

22

Tap TeamViewer Universal Add-On

In the Installed apps list, tap TeamViewer Universal Add-On to open its settings.

23

Turn On the Add-On

The add-on is currently Off. Tap the toggle to turn it On.

24

Allow Full Control

Android asks if you want to Allow TeamViewer Universal Add-On to have full control. Tap Allow to grant access.

25

Connection Active — You're All Set!

Your screen is now being shared. The DigiKaveri support person can see your screen and will guide you from here. You'll see the QuickSupport icon in the taskbar at the bottom.
