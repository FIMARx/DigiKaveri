1. General

By ordering a service from DigiKaveri, the customer accepts these terms. The service is intended for consumers and small businesses in Finland. We reserve the right to change these terms; the valid terms can always be found on our website.

2. Provision of Service

We offer IT support either via remote connection (all of Finland) or on-site as a home visit (Uusimaa area).

Remote Support: Implemented using secure remote support software. The connection is established only with the customer's permission, and the customer can see everything done on the device at all times.

Home Visit: Agreed with the customer in advance. The customer or their representative must be present during the provision of the service.

3. "No Fix, No Fee" and Satisfaction Guarantee

We want to offer a risk-free service. The "No fix, no fee" guarantee means the following:

If we cannot solve your problem or provide a clear workaround or solution proposal, the service is completely free of charge for you.

Exception (Hardware Failures): If the cause of the problem is found to be broken hardware (e.g., broken hard drive or motherboard) that cannot be repaired via software, the diagnosis of the fault is considered a successful job, charged according to the normal price list. We will then advise the customer on further measures.

Payment method is an invoice (email or paper invoice). The payment term is 14 days net unless otherwise agreed.
